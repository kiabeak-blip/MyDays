Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MyDays - Family Tasks ("the App") is developed by HnN Developers. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

Account & Authentication

The App uses Firebase Authentication to create your account. We collect your email address (or Google account information if you sign in with Google) to identify your account and link it to your family's data. This information is handled by Google Firebase and is not sold or shared with third parties.

Family & Task Data

The App stores the information you and your family create — family member names, avatars, colours, tasks, descriptions, checklists, and task completion records — in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore). This data is only accessible to members of your family group and is used solely to provide the App's task management features.

AI Assistant (Optional)

The App includes an optional AI assistant feature powered by Anthropic's Claude API. If you choose to use this feature, you must provide your own Anthropic API key, which is stored only on your device (not on our servers). When you send a message to the assistant, your message along with relevant family/task context is sent directly to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This feature is entirely optional and not required to use the App.

Local Storage

Settings such as your selected language, theme/appearance preferences, and your Anthropic API key (if provided) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Children's Data

The App is designed for family use and may include profiles for children created and managed by a parent or guardian. No data is collected directly from children; all account creation and data management is performed by the parent/guardian account holder.
